Meimay is a set of free, browser-only chemistry learning tools. No install, no sign-up; your progress is saved on your own device. Pick a trainer to begin.

IUPAC naming

Meimay Naming

Practise naming organic compounds with IUPAC nomenclature: flashcards, type-in and step-by-step quizzes, a draw-the-structure reverse mode, and R/S assignment. Both current and pre-1993 locant styles are accepted.

IR · NMR · MS

Meimay Spectra

Read IR, ¹H/¹³C-NMR and MS spectra to deduce structures. Learn how characteristic bands and chemical shifts behave, sharpen reflexes in focused drills, then rebuild unknowns from fragments in full analysis mode.

Reaction mechanisms

Meimay Arrows

Draw the curly arrows yourself and build organic reaction mechanisms step by step: start an arrow at a bond or a lone pair and push electrons through SN1, SN2, E2, carbonyl additions and more.

About Meimay

Meimay is an independent, non-commercial educational project built by Shintaro Shimizu and Jumpei Toshimori — the learning tools we wished existed. Everything runs in your browser, no account is needed, and your progress is stored only in your device's local storage and is never sent to any server.

Each trainer follows standard references but includes representations simplified for learning. For details on data handling and limitations, see the Privacy Policy and Disclaimer inside each app.
