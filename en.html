About Meimay

Meimay is an independent, non-commercial educational project built by Shintaro Shimizu and Jumpei Toshimori — the learning tools we wished existed. Everything runs in your browser, no account is needed, and your progress is stored only in your device's local storage and is never sent to any server.

Each trainer follows standard references but includes representations simplified for learning. For details on data handling and limitations, see the Privacy Policy and Disclaimer inside each app.
